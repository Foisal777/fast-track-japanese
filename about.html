Our Story

About Fast Track

A decade of excellence in Japanese language education in Bangladesh.

Who We Are

Bangladesh's Most Trusted Japanese Centre

Fast Track Japanese Language Centre was founded in 2014 with a single mission: to give Bangladeshi students the fastest, most effective pathway to Japanese language fluency.

Over the past decade, we have trained more than 2,000 students — many of whom now work, study, and thrive in Japan. Our graduates have secured positions at leading Japanese companies and earned MEXT scholarships.

We are not just a language school. We are a gateway to Japan. Our Fast Track Method combines spaced repetition, immersive practice, and exam-focused preparation.
